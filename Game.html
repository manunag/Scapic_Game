Number Game

The rules of the game are:

  • When the game starts, you have 1000 INR.
  • Every time you press start, 3 random numbers are genereated, 100 INR gets deducted.
  • If the random numbers are either even or odd, you get 300 INR.
  • If the numbers are in sequene with difference of 1 in any order (eg: 123,012,... etc), you get 800 INR.
  • If all the random number are same, you get 1000 INR.
  • If INR reaches 0, Gave Over.
